Is there a Destiny Trials Report app?

Coming soon to iOS and Android! For the time being, you can achieve a native app feeling by adding this webpage to your homescreen.

Why did my flawless count not update?

Flawless count is based on visits to the Lighthouse per character per week. Only one visit per character, per week is counted.

Why don't you support other game modes like Skirmish, for example?

Bungie does not let us see the current fireteam you're matched with. We're considering it, but it would likely require manual entry of player names.

What data do you store on your servers?

We store Trials of Osiris data, including individual players, the matches they played, and the weapons they used.
